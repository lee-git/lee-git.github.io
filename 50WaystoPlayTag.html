Tag is an amazing, high energy group game that just about everyone knows how to play. This book is a collection of variant rules that will bring new life to an old favorite.

Did you know there are local twists on tag in many different countries? We have also written some brand-new versions for this book! There is a section for zero-preparation variants that you can play anytime, a section for variants that need a special field or boundary lines, and a section for variants that need supplies.

Included are 50 sets of game directions in clear language so that you can get your group playing quickly.
